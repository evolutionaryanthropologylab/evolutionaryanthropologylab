Evolutionary Anthropology Lab at University of Iowa

Biological anthropology with a computational approach

The Evolutionary Anthropology Lab applies phylogenetic methods to key questions in biological anthropology, ranging from human-virus interactions such as SARS-CoV-2, aDNA of dogs of the Americas, language relatedness, gene selection in primates and more!

Check out the research page to read more about current/past projects, and our members page to see current/past members and their biographies.
